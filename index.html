Discogs Seller Locator

A browser extension for finding shared Discogs sellers.

Add exact Discogs releases or master releases to a stack, then scan marketplace pages you can access in your browser to find sellers with every item.

  1. Clone or download this repository.
  2. Open your browser extension settings and enable developer mode.
  3. Load this folder as an unpacked extension.
  4. Open Discogs release or master pages and click Add to Seller Locator.
